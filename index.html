Frequently Questions

Discover our travelers' main questions

Do I need a visa for the Maldives?

No. Travelers from most countries (including the U.S., Canada, UK, EU, and Brazil) receive a free 30-day tourist visa on arrival.

When is the best time to visit?

November to April: Sunny skies, calm seas (perfect for diving/snorkeling). Avoid May–October (rainy season), though prices are lower.

How much does a trip cost?

Budget: From $1,500 USD (guesthouses, local islands). Luxury: 5,000–15,000+ USD for 7 nights (overwater villas, all-inclusive resorts).
